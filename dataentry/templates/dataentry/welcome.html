Welcome to the web pages of the Pro-Government Militias Database (PGMD) Project. You can browse for information on pro-government militias, their types, members, targets, purpose, support, and countries using the menu options on the left side of the screen.

Initial funding was received from the Economic and Social Research Council (ESRC), UK, RES-062-23-0363, and the Centre for the Study of Civil War (CSCW) at the Peace Research Institute Oslo (PRIO).

Main Investigators: Sabine C. Carey and Neil J. Mitchell. Contact us here.

Advance notice of update PGMD version 2.0
We are currently working on updating the PGMD version 2.0. Version 2.0 will cover the years 1981 to 2014 and will include new variables on how PGMs were terminated, how they were created, as well as more specific information on ethnic membership and ethnic targets. You can view a DRAFT of the codebook for the updated PGMD v2.0 here.

PLEASE NOTE that this codebook will continue to change until the release of PGMD version 2.0, which is planned for 2019.
